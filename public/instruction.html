Description

To calculate the maximum duration of a period fill out the form "Input Data" and click on the button Calculate

When the calculation is completed you will see the latest, legal estimated time of arrival (ETA) for the rotation that you described

Inputs for inflight rest and not acclimatised crew members will be implemented soon
